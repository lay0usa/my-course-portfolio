# Pancake Recipe

## Ingredients

- Flour
- Milk
- Eggs
- Sugar
- Butter

## Steps

1. Mix all the ingredients together.
2. Heat a pan with butter.
3. Pour the pancake mix slowly.
4. Cook both sides until golden.

# Seneca Academic Integrity Policy

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to explain the importance of academic integrity at Seneca and help students understand academic honesty.

## Academic Integrity Definition

> Academic integrity means being honest and fair in academic work and giving proper credit to other people’s ideas and work.

## Policy Sections

- **General**
- **Academic Integrity Program**
- **Responsibilities**
- **Sanctions**

## 1. General

Students are expected to complete their own work honestly and avoid cheating, plagiarism, or copying.

## 2. Academic Integrity Program

The academic integrity program helps students understand the rules and responsibilities related to academic honesty.

## 3. Responsibilities

### Student Responsibilities

- Complete their own work
- Follow Seneca rules
- Ask for help when needed

### Faculty Responsibilities

- Support students
- Explain academic integrity clearly
- Follow proper procedures

## 4. Sanctions

Sanctions are consequences for breaking academic integrity rules.

Possible sanctions include:

- Warning
- Grade reduction
- Failing an assignment
- Suspension
- Expulsion

## Procedure Table

| Violation | Possible Consequence |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Cheating | Warning or grade penalty |
| Plagiarism | Failing assignment |
| Repeated Violations | Suspension |
